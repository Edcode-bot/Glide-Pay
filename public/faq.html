General Questions

GlidePay is a comprehensive financial services platform designed for Uganda. It offers various services including money transfers, bill payments, online shopping, investments, and savings products. Our platform makes managing your money easier and more accessible.

You can add money to your GlidePay wallet through several methods:
  • Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Card Payment
  • Crypto Transfer (CELO)
Simply go to your wallet page and click on "Add Funds" to see all available options.
Security

Yes, your data is fully secure with GlidePay. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. Our platform is regularly audited and complies with all relevant financial regulations.

To reset your PIN:
  1. Go to Profile
  2. Click on "Set PIN"
  3. Select "Forgot PIN"
  4. Follow the verification process
  5. Set your new PIN
For additional security, you may need to verify your identity through your registered phone number or email.
Transactions

GlidePay supports various digital wallets including:
  • MetaMask
  • Valora
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet
We're continuously adding support for more wallets to provide better accessibility.
